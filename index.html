About

The ISSR Programming Club (a.k.a. the ISSR Coders) is a club at ISSR that aims to teach the students of ISSR how to code in several different programming languages. At the moment, we have 4 courses planned:

General Information

The ISSR Coders is set to begin on the 21st of November, 2017. To register, contact issrcoders@gmail.com with the subject "ISSR Coders Registration". You can also contact us there if you have any questions.

The ISSR Coders will be held from 15:10 to 16:10 every Tuesday in the MYP Hub unless informed otherwise.
