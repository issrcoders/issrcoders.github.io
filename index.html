About Us

The ISSR Coders are a club at ISSR that wants to teach sudents at ISSR how to code!

We teach Scratch, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python. Meaning we will teach you how to make websites and video games!

Every week we have lessons from 3:05 to 4:05 in the school library, you can come or leave at anytime and the lessons are free and you don't need to sign up.
