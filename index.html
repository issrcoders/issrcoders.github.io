ISSR Coders

About

The ISSR Programming Club (a.k.a. the ISSR Coders) is a club at ISSR that aims to teach the students of ISSR how to code. At the moment, we have 3 courses planned:

COURSE NAME Scratch Python 3 Web Development
DIFFICULTY Beginner Intermediate Intermediate

General Information

The ISSR Coders is set to begin on the 21st of November, 2017. To register, contact issrcoders@gmail.com with the subject "ISSR Coders Registration".

The ISSR Coders will be held at 15:10 every Tuesday in the MYP Hub unless informed otherwise.
